Frequently Asked Questions

Dhaka experiences a tropical monsoon climate, with the summer months (March to June) seeing temperatures up to 97°F (36°C), and the winter months (November to February) experiencing milder temperatures around 70°F (21°C). The monsoon season (June to October) brings heavy rainfall.
Most travelers prefer to visit Dhaka between November and February, when the weather is mild and dry. During this time, the Ekushey Book Fair and Victory Day celebrations are popular local events.
Travelers often visit the National Parliament House, a marvel of architectural design, and the Liberation War Museum, which preserves the history of Bangladesh's struggle for independence. The Dhakeshwari Temple and the Star Mosque are also frequently suggested.
Families in Dhaka can spend a day at the Bangladesh National Zoo, explore the educational exhibits at the Bangladesh National Museum, or enjoy picnics and boat rides at Gulshan Lake Park. The Fantasy Kingdom amusement park is also a favorite among kids.
